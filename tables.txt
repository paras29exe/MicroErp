Explanation (why these exist)

# Core tables

users → login + roles

products → raw materials and finished goods

customers → sales customers

vendors → suppliers

# Purchase module

purchases → purchase invoice header

purchase_items → products inside that purchase

# Sales module

sales → sales invoice header

sales_items → items sold in the invoice

# Production module

bom → finished product recipe

bom_items → raw materials required

production → manufacturing record

# Finance

expenses → manual expenses

# Inventory

inventory_transactions → history of stock movement
(purchase, sale, production, adjustment)
